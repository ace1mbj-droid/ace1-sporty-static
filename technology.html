Terahertz Wave Technology

A design approach focused on comfort and everyday wear

What is Terahertz Technology?

Terahertz waves exist between microwave and infrared radiation on the electromagnetic spectrum, typically in the frequency range of 0.1 to 10 THz. At Ace#1, we use this concept as inspiration for how we think about materials, comfort, and everyday wear.

Our approach focuses on practical shoe design: cushioning, support, and durability—built for daily movement.

What this means for you:

  • Comfort-focused cushioning
  • Balanced support for daily wear
  • Lightweight feel
  • Breathable materials
  • Built for everyday movement

Note: Our footwear is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is terahertz radiation safe?

Ace#1 shoes are designed for everyday wear and comfort. We do not market our products as medical devices and we do not make medical claims.

How long before I see results?

Comfort and fit are personal and can vary by use case. Most customers notice the fit and feel immediately, and preferences can change as you break them in.

Do I need to charge the shoes?

No charging required—our shoes work like normal footwear and require only regular care and cleaning.

Can anyone use terahertz shoes?

Our shoes are designed for general everyday use. If you have a medical condition or specific foot concerns, consider consulting a qualified professional for footwear advice.
