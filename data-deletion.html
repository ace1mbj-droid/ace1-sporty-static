We respect your right to privacy and control over your personal data. This page allows you to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal information from our systems.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and password
  • Profile Data: Profile picture, preferences, and settings
  • Addresses: Saved shipping and billing addresses
  • Wishlist: All items in your wishlist
  • Reviews: Product reviews and ratings you've submitted
  • Session Data: All active login sessions
  • Social Login Data: Google/Facebook OAuth connections

What Data Will Be Retained?

For legal, tax, and fraud prevention purposes, we may retain certain information:

  • Order History: Anonymized transaction records (for up to 7 years for tax compliance)
  • Payment Records: Basic payment transaction IDs (no credit card details)
  • Legal Communications: Records required by law or for dispute resolution
üìå Note: Retained data will be anonymized (all personally identifiable information removed) and cannot be linked back to you.

Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated immediately upon request
  • Within 30 days: All personal data will be permanently deleted from our active systems
  • Within 90 days: Data removed from backup systems

Alternative Options

If you're concerned about privacy but don't want to delete your account, consider:

  • Update Your Information: Edit or remove specific data in your profile settings
  • Deactivate Account: Temporarily disable your account instead of deleting it

  • Clear Wishlist/Reviews: Manually remove specific items or reviews
