How to Measure Your Foot

Follow these simple steps to find your perfect fit:

1

Prepare

Place a blank piece of paper on a hard floor against a wall. Wear the socks you'll typically wear with the shoes.

2

Position

Stand on the paper with your heel against the wall. Keep your weight evenly distributed.

3

Mark

Have someone mark the longest part of your foot on the paper, or do it yourself with a pencil held vertically.

4

Measure

Measure the distance from the wall to the mark in centimeters. This is your foot length.

Sizing Tips & Recommendations

  • Measure your feet at the end of the day when they're at their largest
  • If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for better comfort
  • Always measure both feet and use the larger measurement
  • ACE#1 shoes run true to size based on the measurements above
  • Allow about 0.5-1 cm of space between your longest toe and the shoe end
  • For athletic shoes, consider sizing up half a size for better performance
  • Still unsure? Contact our customer support for personalized assistance
