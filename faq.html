Terahertz technology uses electromagnetic waves that promote better blood circulation, reduce inflammation, and enhance overall foot health. Our shoes are embedded with terahertz chips that emit beneficial frequencies to support your wellness throughout the day.
Are ACE#1 shoes suitable for daily wear?
Yes! ACE#1 shoes are designed for everyday use. They combine wellness technology with comfort and style, making them perfect for work, casual outings, exercise, and daily activities.

The terahertz chips embedded in ACE#1 footwear are designed to last for the lifetime of the shoe. They require no batteries or charging and maintain their effectiveness throughout regular use.
